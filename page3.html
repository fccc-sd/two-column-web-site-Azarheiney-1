Slenderman is a mystery book about a college student. His friend goes missing and he starts having nightmares about where she could be so he makes a post about it on reddit. Some of the comments on reddit tell him that slenderman is coming for him and if he tries to find his friend, and that he shouldn't look for her. If he looks for her and manages to bring her back, he won't be able to come back. While other comments are excited about how the story will turn out. He decides to look for her anyways, and ends up with a friend to help share his story if he doesn't come back. He saves his friend but he doesn't come back with her and everyone is confused as to where his friend was. His friend doesn't even know where she was, and the friend he made over email decides to share the last recording of him onto reddit. Some of comments on the post are saying that they warned him, while others are upset at how the story ended. His classmates don't relize he is missing until his friend tries to text him to tell him that their friend is back.

They Both Die at The End is a sad book about two boys, Mateo and Rufus, getting a call from death-cast, which can predict the day that people will die, then calls them to let them know so they can live their last day to the fullest. A couple chapters in Mateo and Rufus end up meeting and they spend their last day together. The two go to a resturant to try new food, a place where they can experince different things, after doing the experince together they go to the park and talk for a little bit. Mateo and Rufus talk about how Rufuss' family got the call on the same day exept for him, and they all past away in a car crash. Eventually they end up at Mateos apartment and thats where they hang out for the rest of the day, Mateo woke up from his nap and went to cook a snack for Rufus forgetting that his stove is broken and it catches on fire. Near the end Rufus writes Mateos' dad a letter letting him know that when he wakes up his son got out of the house and did a lot of different things that he would not normally have done without Rufus to push that little bit to get him out of his shell.
